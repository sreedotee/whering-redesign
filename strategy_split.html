The Navigation Pivot

Minimalist 3-Tab

Standard approach for MVP utility apps. Focuses on core inventory.

  • ✕ Low discoverability for secondary features.
  • ✕ No dedicated space for "Social Feedback".
  • ✕ Search buried inside tabs (High friction).
STATUS: REJECTED
REASON: Too restrictive for Whering's future commercial & social scale.
WINNER

Symmetric 5

Premium "Destinational" approach. Balances creation and consumption.

  • ✓ Center Action: Maximal thumb reach for 'Studio'.
  • ✓ Explore Tab: Dedicated hub for Brands/Trends.
  • ✓ Social Hub: Real-time feedback via Inbox tab.
  • ✓ Mental Room: No semantic pollution between tabs.
STATUS: ADOPTED
REASON: Provides the real-estate needed for a "Power User" experience.
